About Your Journey Forward

After surviving childhood trauma, many people find themselves at a crossroads: they've done the hard work of healing, but now what? How do you build a life that reflects who you truly are, not who trauma tried to make you?

I'm a certified life coach specializing in working with adult survivors who are ready for their next chapter. I believe that your past experiences, while part of your story, don't have to define your future.

My approach is strength-based and forward-focused. We don't revisit trauma - instead, we harness the resilience you've already developed and channel it toward creating meaningful goals, building confidence, and designing a life that feels authentically yours.
